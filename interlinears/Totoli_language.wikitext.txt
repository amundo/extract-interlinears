'''Totoli''' (also known as '''Tolitoli''') is a [[Celebic languages|Sulawesi]] language of the [[Austronesian languages|Austronesian language]] family spoken by 25,000 people of [[Central Sulawesi]], [[Indonesia]].

==Grammar==

===Voice===

Totoli has a [[Austronesian alignment|symmetrical voice]] system.<ref name="Riesberg">{{cite book |last1=Riesberg |first1=Sonja |title=Symmetrical voice and linking in Western Austronesian languages |publisher=Walter de Gruyter |date=2014}}</ref> In the examples below, ''i Winarno mongusut kunji motorna'' is an actor voice construction, while ''kunji itu kusuti i Winarno.'' is an undergoer voice construction.

{{interlinear|indent=3
|I Winaro moN-kusut kunji motor{{=}}na
|HON PN AV-look.for key scooter{{=}}3S.GEN
|'Winarno is looking for the keys of his scooter.'<ref name="Riesberg" />{{rp|1}}}}

{{interlinear|indent=3
|kunji itu kusut-i i Winaro
|key DIST look.for-UV2 HON PN
|'The keys are being looked for by Winarno.'<ref name="Riesberg" />{{rp|1}}}}

The syntactic pivot can be placed before the predicate, as shown:

{{interlinear|indent=3
|gaukan no-gutu ponguman itu
|king AV.RLS-make story DIST
|'The king made this announcement.'<ref name="AdelaarHimmelmann">{{cite book |editor1-last=Adelaar |editor1-first=K. Alexander |editor2-last=Himmelmann |editor2-first=Nikolaus |title=The Austronesian languages of Asia and Madagascar |date=2005}}</ref>{{rp|142}}}}

{{interlinear|indent=3
|kopi ia ni-pogutu i Andris
|coffee PRX RLS(UG)-make PN Andrew
|'Andrew made this coffee.'<ref name="AdelaarHimmelmann" />{{rp|143}}}}
