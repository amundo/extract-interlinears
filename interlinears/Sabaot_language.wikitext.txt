'''Sabaot''' (''Sebei'') is a [[Kalenjin language]] of [[Kenya]]. The [[Sabaot people]] live around [[Mount Elgon]] in both Kenya and [[Uganda]]. The hills of their homeland gradually rise from an elevation of 5,000 to 14,000 feet. The [[Kenya]]–[[Uganda]] border goes straight through the mountain-top, cutting the Sabaot homeland into two halves.<ref>https://joshuaproject.net/assets/media/profiles/text/t14623_ke.pdf</ref>

==Grammar==
