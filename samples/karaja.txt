Iny		boho	kdùra	rkiròrènykre.
/idə̃		boho	kədʊɾa	ɾək-ɪ-ɾɔ=ɾ-ɛdə̃=kɾe/

‘We (inclusive) will eat fish.’

In addition, the latter example shows how the pluralizer langkpj–boho, when combined with the noun for people (langkpjiny), functions as a first person plural inclusive pronoun to include those outside of a specific group. According to Ribeiro, langkpjiny serves the same function as the phrase langpta gente, commonly found in [[Brazilian Portuguese]].
