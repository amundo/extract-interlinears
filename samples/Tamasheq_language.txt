==== Compounding ====
Tamashek makes use of [[Compound (linguistics)compounding]] to form nouns. Most noun-noun compounds necessitate a possessor [[Preposition and postpositionpreposition]] ə̀n in between the two morphemes, which can be analytically structured as [X [ə̀n Y]] 'X of Y.' Depending on the nouns, ə̀n may become unaccented, as shown in the first example below.
